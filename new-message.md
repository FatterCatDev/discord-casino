Inventory System Update (Design Phase)

The Inventory System is currently in design and is a work in progress.

Planned direction:
- Every player will have an inventory.
- Items will be purchasable through /shop.
- Items will affect bet outcomes and settlement behavior in eligible gambling games.
- Items will not change game RNG or core fairness logic.

Planned MVP item:
- Double Next Bet (Free): your next eligible bet is treated as 2x for payout calculation.
- If the bet loses, only the original stake is lost.
- If the bet wins, payout is calculated from the doubled effective bet.

Status: not live yet. Implementation, balancing, and testing are in progress.
